# Privacy Policy — Listenr

_Last updated: July 5, 2026_

Listenr is a Chrome extension that reads the text of the web page you are viewing aloud using your device's built-in text-to-speech engine.

## What Listenr collects

**Nothing.** Listenr does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any user data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no external servers.

## What Listenr stores (locally only)

Listenr saves the following on your own device using Chrome's local storage:

- Your settings (voice, playback speed, toggles)
- Your last playback position for pages you've listened to

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the extension.

## How Listenr accesses pages

When you start playback, Listenr reads the visible text of the current page in order to speak it aloud and highlight the sentence being spoken. This happens entirely on your device. Page content is never recorded, stored beyond the playback position, or transmitted anywhere.

## Speech synthesis

Text-to-speech is performed by your operating system's built-in speech engine (e.g. Samantha on macOS). No text is sent to any third-party service by Listenr.
